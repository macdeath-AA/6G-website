Roles

  • Research in 5G-NR PHY/L2/L3 and development of algorithms for RAN & UE.
  • Identify new research trends and perform research on the same.
  • Contribute to the integration with enabling technologies.
  • Author and contribute to software design, development, validation, and documentation to deliver high-quality products in the world of 5G.
  • Publish research papers at prestigious venues and journals.
  • Submit contributions to standard organization meetings and file patents.

Preferred Skills

  • Good understanding of Signal Processing and Wireless communication.
  • Strong programming skills with C/C++, python, MATLAB.
  • Software design and development experience in wireless domain.
  • Experience with industry standard software development tools: code revision systems (git, etc),IDEs and build tools.
  • Understanding of 3GPP LTE/5G specifications.
