ICPS 5G Advance ORAN Massive MIMO Base Station

This project will be focusing on enabling the design and development of fundamental technology building blocks of 5G-advanced (5G+) &6G systems and networks. The prime focus will be on indigenous cutting-edge research leading to intellectual property (IP) generation that includes product IP for commercial usage and development of patents (IPR) that will not only enable product-oriented innovation but also target adoption into the upcoming 5G advanced and 6G systems. It will also have above related other advanced communication systems R&D which has commercial potential.

The objective of this project is to the development of world-class ORAN Massive MIMO base station. Also, to train about 85 highly skilled R&D engineers over the duration of the project, who will develop world's leading 5G advanced base station. Also, to develop O- RAN fronthaul interfaces and RU. The RU design includes the digital front end (DFE) design and the parts of the PHY layer as per Split Option 7-2X, as well as the digital beamforming functionality. To have a significant focus on clean-slate design of L2 scheduler and stack whereas the other components/sub-systems such as PHY, RU and Core may be developed in collaboration with partners. The project will be establishing a collaboration with research groups working in this area both in India and abroad

5G+/6G Converged Terrestrial and Satellite IoT (5G+/6G-sIoT)

This project aim is to develop radio communication technologies that can potentially form the backbone of 6G IoT technologies. The project aims to develop prototype products leading to commercialization of Converged Terrestrial and Satellite Communication and IoT technologies.

The project aims to develop prototypes for Converged Terrestrial and Satellite Communication based IoT prototype products. This prototype supports Satellite communication as well as Internet-of-Things (IoT) modems that are implemented using a combination of 5G cellular, and 5G+ and 6G Satcom standards or proposed standards. Also, to develop IoT enabled base station (BS) and User Equipment (UE) prototype product based on next generation 5G cellular systems and 5G+/6G Satellite system. Further, integrated NB- IoT cellular and Satcom chipsets based on 5G NB IoT and 5G+/6G Satcom NB-IoT. The prototype products developed will be tested using lab/field trails before delivering them to manufacturers and chipset vendors.

Next Generation Wireless Research and Standardization on 5G and Beyond

The aim of the project is to conduct research in areas which areas part of 5G and beyond, the research outputs will be filed as patents, publications. Standardization of all relevant research outcomes will be taken to the Indian standards forum TSDSI and the global standard forums like 3GPP, ITU and IEEE to incorporate it as SEPs. Also, building 5G and Beyond Solutions i.e. developing appropriate wireless systems and subsystems for Indian defense and telecom equipment vendors in India. Implementing the relevant algorithms developed through this project in the 5G testbed and building hundreds of experts in the area of 5G and beyond technologies in various Industries, Research and Development organizations.

This project is envisaged to contribute to address:
a. Technology -building industrial and R&D capacity in the design and IP dimensions of 5G;
b. Manufacturing - expanding the manufacturing base in 5G.
The project outcome is to create a pool of researchers and designers who will continue to build products for India Also, to provide technological know-how to a large community of professionals in Indian product and service companies. Further, to provide an opportunity to the Bachelor Program students (&500 no's) to work on the 5G and beyond technologies as and when it is developed and will get a hands-on experience on the technology by leveraging the indigenous 5G testbed being built by some of the participating testbed.
The net result of the above activities is that it will promote manufacturing in India, develop solutions for Indian broadband requirements, and build deep technical skills in the country apart from reducing the import bills in the sector, and more importantly generates high quality jobs in the core sector and contribute significantly to the GDP, and for the security needs of the country.
